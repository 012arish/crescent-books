About Crescent Books

Established in 1991, Crescent Books has been a beacon of knowledge in Aligarh. We are dedicated to publishing high-quality educational material that inspires curiosity and fosters a love for learning.

Our mission is to provide accessible, engaging, and culturally rich literature for students of all ages. From Hindi stories to academic texts, we take pride in our heritage and our commitment to the future generation.
